About Us

We are the Chiang Mai Hiking Club, dedicated to promoting healthy outdoor activities and building a community of nature enthusiasts.

Weekly Hikes

Join us for our regular weekly hikes to explore the beautiful Chiang Mai countryside. It's a great way to stay active, improve your fitness, and make new friends who share your passion for hiking.

Multi-Day Excursions

If you're looking for a more extended adventure, we also organize multi-day excursions. These excursions allow you to immerse yourself in nature and experience the thrill of multi-day hikes in stunning locations.

Benefits of Hiking

  • Improved fitness and stamina
  • Connection with nature
  • Stress relief and mental well-being
  • Opportunity to meet like-minded individuals
